Air Fryer Broiled Grapefruit

Description

This warm and juicy grapefruit with a buttery sweet topping is the perfect accompaniment to your Sunday brunch!

Consider adding just a pinch of sea salt at the end to enhace the flavor!

Ingredients

  • Red grapefruit (refrigerated)
  • Softened butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 400 degrees F
  2. Cut grapefruit in half crosswise (You may need to slice off a slice of the bottom of each half to ensure the fruit sits flat and doesn't roll)
  3. Combine softened butter and brown sugar in a small bowl
  4. Spread butter/sugar mixture over the grapefruit halves and sprinkle additional brown sugar on top
  5. Place each grapefruit half into a square of aluminum foil with the edges turned up to catch any juices and place in the air fryer
  6. Broil in the air fryer until the sugar mixture is bubbling (Usually 6 or 7 minutes)
  7. Sprinkle fruit with cinnamon and enjoy!
