Pizza Sandwich

Description

The pizza sandwich is a fun and unique way to get the taste of pizza without all of the calories!

This super tasty treat will be sure to tickle your fancy!

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Pizza sauce
  • Laughing Cow cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Spread the laughing cow cheese onto the bread (I like to use two slices of cheese)
  2. Spoon desired amount of pizza sauce onto one slice of bread
  3. Arrange pepperoni on top of pizza sauce
  4. Put sandwich halves together
  5. Put sandwich into a pan with heated butter or ghee
  6. Toast until golden brown and then enjoy!
