Sweet Tea

Description

Truly a Southern staple, an ice cold glass of sweet tea can cool you right down even on the hottest of days.

Every true Southern knows - sweet tea must be made from scratch and cold unsweetened tea can never be properly made sweet!

Ingredients

  • Black tea of your choice (I usually use Lipton brand)
  • Granulated white sugar

Steps

  1. Begin by boiling water on your stove
  2. Once water is boiling, remove water from heat and add tea bags to water
  3. Cover pot to trap heat inside
  4. Allow tea to steep for at least 30 minutes
  5. While tea is steeping, pour one cup of sugar into the pitcher (You may wish to add more or less and adjust for sweetness)
  6. Once the time is up, pour the still hot tea into the pitcher and stir for 5 minutes or until sugar is fully dissolved
  7. Enjoy!
